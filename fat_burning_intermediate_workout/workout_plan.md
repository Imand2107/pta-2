# 13-Exercise Fat-Burning Workout Intermediate

## Exercises
1. **Jumping Jacks**
   - Duration: 30 seconds

2. **Mountain Climbers**
   - Reps: 20 reps (10 per leg)

3. **Burpees**
   - Reps: 10-12 reps

4. **High Knees**
   - Duration: 30 seconds

5. **Squat Jumps**
   - Reps: 12-15 reps

6. **Plank to Push-Up**
   - Reps: 8-10 reps

7. **Lunges (Jumping or Alternating)**
   - Reps: 12-15 reps per leg

8. **Bicycle Crunches**
   - Reps: 20 reps (10 per side)

9. **Skater Jumps**
   - Reps: 12-15 reps per side

10. **Plank with Leg Lifts**
    - Reps: 10 reps per leg

11. **Jumping Lunges**
    - Reps: 12 reps per leg

12. **Tricep Dips**
    - Reps: 10-12 reps

13. **Side Plank (Left & Right)**
    - Duration: 20-30 seconds per side
